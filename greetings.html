French Greetings (Les Salutations)

Learn how to say hello, goodbye, and ask “how are you” in French.

Common Greetings

French Pronunciation English Meaning When to Use
Bonjour bon-zhoor Hello / Good morning All day until evening
Bonsoir bon-swahr Good evening After 6 PM
Salut sah-loo Hi / Bye Informal, with friends
Au revoir oh ruh-vwar Goodbye Formal & informal
Bonne nuit bun nwee Good night Before sleeping
À bientôt ah byan-toh See you soon Friendly, casual
À demain ah duh-mã See you tomorrow Casual
Coucoukoo-koo Hey!Very informal
Bienvenue byan-vuh-noo Welcome Greeting someone

Polite Expressions

French Pronunciation English Meaning
Comment ça va ? koh-mahn sah vah How are you? (informal)
Ça va bien sah vah byan I'm fine
Très bien, merci tray byan, mehr-see Very well, thank you
Et toi ? ay twah And you? (informal)
Comment allez-vous ? koh-mahn tah-lay voo How are you? (formal)
Je vais bien zhuh vay byan I'm doing well
Fun Fact: In France, greetings often include la bise (a light kiss on both cheeks) with friends and family. Handshakes are more common in formal settings.
