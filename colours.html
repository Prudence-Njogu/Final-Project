Colours in French

Here are some of the most common colours in French with their pronunciation.

Rouge

(rooʒ) - Red

Bleu

(bluh) - Blue

Vert

(vair) - Green

Jaune

(zhon) - Yellow

Noir

(nwar) - Black

Blanc

(blahn) - White

Orange

(oh-rahnj) - Orange

Rose

(rohz) - Pink

Violet

(vee-oh-lay) - Purple

Marron

(mah-rohn) - Brown

Gris

(gree) - Grey

Tip: In French, colours agree with the gender/number of the noun. Example: "un livre vert" (a green book, masculine) vs "une pomme verte" (a green apple, feminine).
