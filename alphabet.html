The French Alphabet

The French alphabet has the same 26 letters as English, but the pronunciation is different. Mastering it will help you spell names, places, and words correctly in French!

Letter Pronunciation Example Word
A ah ami (friend)
B bay bateau (boat)
C say café (coffee)
D day dimanche (Sunday)
E uh école (school)
F eff famille (family)
G zhay gâteau (cake)
H ahsh hôtel (hotel)
I ee île (island)
J zhee jardin (garden)
K kah kangourou (kangaroo)
L ell livre (book)
M em maison (house)
N en nuit (night)
O oh orange (orange)
P pay pain (bread)
Q koo quatre (four)
R air (rolled) rouge (red)
S ess soleil (sun)
T tay table (table)
U oo (rounded lips) université (university)
V vay voiture (car)
W doo-bluh-vay wagon (wagon)
X eeks taxi (taxi)
Y ee-grec yaourt (yogurt)
Z zed zéro (zero)
Fun Fact: The French R is very different from English — it's produced in the throat. Also, the letter H is always silent in French.
