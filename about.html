About French Basics

French Basics is your beginner-friendly guide to learning essential French words, phrases, and grammar.
Whether you're preparing for travel, studying for school, or just curious about the French language, this site makes learning simple and fun.



Why Learn French?

French is spoken by over 300 million people worldwide and is an official language in more than 25 countries. Learning French opens the door to new cultures, better travel experiences, and more opportunities in education and careers.



What You'll Learn Here

  • The French alphabet and correct pronunciation
  • Everyday greetings and polite expressions
  • Numbers, days, time, and seasons
  • Common words for travel, food, family, and animals
  • Basic grammar rules to build your first sentences


Our Goal

To give you the confidence to start speaking and understanding French, step by step. We believe learning a new language should be fun, easy, and accessible to everyone. Start exploring the lessons and take your first step towards speaking French today!
