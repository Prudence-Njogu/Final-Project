Counting by Tens

Number French Pronunciation
30 trente trawnt
40 quarante kah-rawnt
50 cinquante san-kawnt
60 soixante swah-sawnt
70 soixante-dix swah-sawnt-dees

Quick Tip: French numbers after 69 can look tricky. Example: 72 = soixante-douze (literally "sixty-twelve"). 95 = quatre-vingt-quinze ("four twenties fifteen").
